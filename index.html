RealUSA Travel App

A UX/UI Case Study

RealUSA is a travel app that is simple to use and allows the user a quick method to plan a vacation within the 49 states of the USA that that be accessed by automobile.

After a two-year period consisting of a lock down and worldwide travel restrictions due to a global pandemic; my theory was that a large percentage of the US population would continue to abstain from traveling.

Empathize

Let's get to know our user!

For this step of the project, a user research plan was created that outlined the objectives for what I wanted to learn about people and how they plan travel. I then developed a list of questions and conducted 5 interviews with a mixed group of men and women.

My main objective was to obtain answers to 5 questions:

1. What impact has the pandemic had on the desire to travel, either domestically or internationally?

2. Has the amount of travel increased or decreased in comparison to pre-pandemic levels?

3. Have the choices of travel destinations, mode of travel, or choice of lodgings changed from those made before the onset of COVID?

4. How has travel planning changed; or are the same methods being used?

5. Have the choices of activities and entertainment changed, and in what ways?

A few of the answers I received:

"I want to go to real places, not just tourist attractions."

"I want to visit someplace I haven't been before."

"I'm looking forward to retirement and traveling more."


"I want to go where the people who live there go."

"I go where ever I want to go."

Define

Taking a close look at the answers we got during our interviews gave us a lot of information.

Analyzing the data...

All of the results were collected and the data was grouped to gather the relevant similarities about potential users of a travel app:

*Family make-up

*Likes and dislikes

*Methods of planning trips/vacations

*Current and future travel desires

Our data shows the problem:

Our user needs to easily plan their vacation and create a trip that is both memorable and fun.

Ideate

Reviewing the data to find a solution.

Our interview data provides us with useful information about what users like about current travel planning apps and what features they wish an app would have.

I looked at the features a potential user liked and wanted and compared them to which ones would have the most impact on the user's experience using the app.

To solve our user's problem, we chose the features that would have the most impact on their vacation planning experience.
Design an app that allows users to choose their destination, lodging, and fun activities/entertainment easily and conveniently pay for them all at once.
The User Flow has been created to provide the paths the user would progress through to plan a vacation.
